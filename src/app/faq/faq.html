What to Know

How do I RSVP, and what is the deadline?

Please RSVP by March 19th using the RSVP link on this site. If you have questions, reach out to Treva Weaver at (614) 218-6528.

I can’t attend — what do I do?

We’re going to miss you, and we completely understand. Please submit an RSVP of “No” as soon as you can so we can plan accordingly.

Can I change my RSVP after I submit it?

Yes — please reach out with the update to Treva Weaver at (614) 218-6528.

Are plus–ones included?

If your invitation includes a plus–one, yes. If it doesn’t, we’re keeping the guest list to those named on the invitation due to space.

Where does the ceremony take place?

The ceremony takes place at Brookshire. Please arrive by 5:15 so you have time to park and get seated.

Where does the reception take place?

Both the ceremony and reception take place at Brookshire.

What time does everything start and end?

Guests may start arriving at 5:00pm for the ceremony at 5:30pm. Please plan to arrive a minimum of 15 minutes early to accommodate for parking and getting seated before the ceremony.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is spring chic. We invite you to wear your bright, fun, and happy colors — think fresh, cheerful, and celebration–ready. Men please wear a jacket; with a splash of color or a fun pattern if you are daring. Ladies wear your best floral or colorful cocktail or semiformal attire.

Is the ceremony or reception indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be outdoors, weather permitting. The reception will be indoors.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes! There is free parking at the venue.

Do you have hotel blocks or recommendations?

Because Brookshire is close to several major areas, including Polaris, Lewis Center, and Delaware, there are many hotel options nearby to fit a wide range of preferences and budgets. We have chosen not to reserve a room block so that guests have the flexibility to stay wherever works best for them.

Are there meal choices, and how do I share dietary restrictions?

We have chosen a buffet style dinner, so no need to pick meals beforehand. Please share allergies or dietary restrictions in the RSVP notes so we can coordinate with the caterer.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We ask that phones stay away during the ceremony so everyone stays present. We have an amazing photographer who we are very excited to have capture the moments of the day.
